About Elim Garak

Elim Garak, an exiled Cardassian tailor living on aboard space station Deep Space 9 (Terok Nor). Garak considers his new life at Deep Space 9 intolerable and as a form of torture. The reason for his exiled is unknown, but it has something to do with his mentor Enabran Tain. While living in exile, he befriends Dr. Julian Bashir, the space station chief medical officer and Odo, the chief of security. Upon their first meeting, Bashir suspects Garak of being a spy, but he denies it. Both Odo and Garak share a mutual respect for one another and wish to return home.

More on Garak...

"Garak changes from a simple mysterious character to one of complexity and secrecy. Again, with the iceberg analogy, the substance of Garak is what you don't hear. It's what he doesn't say." -- Andrew J. Robinson
